WHY VIRET EXISTS

Viret is Latin: “it flourishes.”

Our conviction is that AI's highest use is expanding what people are able to do and to be. The organizations closest to that work — foundations, ministries, global-health and agriculture institutions — deserve the best builders in the world, working in their interest.

So our top-line measure is expanded human capability, reported in the terms decision-makers already use: mission KPIs, cost-effectiveness, service coverage, workforce productivity, development indices — not model benchmarks. That applies both to the staff who run these institutions and to the teachers, farmers, and community health workers they reach.

THE SHIFT

The bottleneck is no longer the model.

Mission organizations want outcomes from AI at speed and scale — not decks and demos. They have lived the alternative: strategy engagements that end at the recommendation, and pilots that fail to scale. What they can't buy is a partner who builds, deploys, and sustains AI inside real operations — and does it without taking ownership of their data, systems, or future. The constraint has moved from model capability to builder capacity that leaves institutions stronger and more independent, not more dependent. That is the gap we close.

HOW IT WORKS IN PRACTICE
Your knowledge, in plain files.

Institutional knowledge lives in human-readable files, in your own repository. Staff read and edit it like a document — not trapped in a vendor's database — and it is yours forever.

Agents propose. People approve.

When an agent learns something worth keeping, it proposes the change; a person you designate reviews and approves it — the same discipline software teams use to protect production code.

One record: audit, evals, evidence.

Every agent action is logged to an append-only record in your store — the audit trail, the evaluation dataset, and the impact evidence are the same artifact. Nothing is a black box.

Open, and sovereign where it counts.

Open-source and open-weight is our starting default — inspectable, sustainable, yours. But for some clients and use-cases, proprietary SaaS and closed models are simply the better tool: convenience, maintenance, capability. We have no stake in either answer — we find what's best for you, across both worlds.

Human-in-the-loop by design.

Every deployment ships with an autonomy dial you set, so the system augments people and keeps high-stakes decisions under human control. Autonomy is a setting, not the pitch.
